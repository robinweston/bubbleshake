Welcome to Bubble Shake!

Bubble Shake was founded in 2026 by Bluebell Heartwing right here in Singapore. Inspired by the way Shake Shack crafts their milkshakes — especially their chocolate milkshake — Bluebell knew she had to create a place that brought that same creamy magic together with the fun of bubble tea. Every recipe on our menu has been taste-tested by her friends before making the cut, so you know each drink has been perfected with honest opinions and plenty of laughs.
